Our Services

We provide expert guidance and execution for complex financial transactions, ensuring optimal outcomes for our clients.

Loan Syndication

Our team specializes in structuring and arranging syndicated loans, connecting businesses with a consortium of lenders to secure large-scale financing with competitive terms and interest rates.

Debt Restructuring

We assist companies in renegotiating their existing debt to improve liquidity, reduce financial burden, and create a sustainable capital structure that supports long-term growth and stability.

About LoanLogic

Established 23 years ago, LoanLogic was founded on the principle of providing strategic, client-centric financial solutions. With our headquarters in Coimbatore and branch offices in Chennai and Mumbai, we have become a pivotal player in India's financial landscape. Our commitment to excellence has enabled us to successfully handle over 12,000 cases of loan syndication and debt restructuring.
